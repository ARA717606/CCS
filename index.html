About Us

CareCore Services is a student-led organization aimed to bridge the gap in educational resources regarding preventive care.

CareCore Services seeks to promote a healthy lifestyle by emphasizing the importance of preventive healthcare amongst underserved communities. Because our organization believes that knowledge is a major part of the solution, we strive to educate these communities through making resources available for all to access.
